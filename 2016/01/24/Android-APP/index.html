Mobile Cartography Project Work - Dresden Zoo

Furui Sun and Shuoyan Huang, Chiyuan Gu

1. Purpose and target group

Our purpose is to develop an Android mobile application for the Dresden Zoo, which can provide information about animals as well as assisst navigation. The target group includes both locals and tourists who visit the Dresden zoo.

2. 2. Functions of the application

When you open the application, you will see the welcome screen, after this screen, the app will jump to the “introduction” screen. At the bottom of the screen is the main menu, which consists 4 buttons: Introduction, Map, Gallery and About.

Introduction: shows a brief introduction of the zoo, its opening times and admission fees.

Map: shows the zoo area. On the top-right, different base maps can be chosen (standard google map or satellite image). A 3D-looking base map drawn by ourselves is overlaid on top of the google map.

At the top-left, users can choose different points of interest (Animal, Restaurant, or Toilet).

When the Animal button is chosen, animal markers will show on the map; when the marker is clicked, an info window will pop up. And when users click the info window, they will be taken to a new screen with detailed information and images about the chosen animal.

For the markers, we stored the relevant information in the database, like adding animal markers, we would first get the icon, text and image information from the database, and show it in the windows.


There is also an “Animal list” button at the bottom left, users can see all animals listed there. When users click animals in the list, they can also be navigated to the detail information window.

Gallery: a horizontal ScrollView is applied with Imageviews, showing animal images taken by ourselves.

About: shows information about the app, such as current version, authors and attributions.

Final remark: During the process of coding, to activate other activities, all need to use the intent to link two activities, like change from main map to other parts of the App. We also tried to only use one activity to show all window information (query data in the main map activity and pass them to the windows activity with using of intent), but there seems something wrong with the database query in the main map activity, so we did not implement this part (we made some comments about this in the code).
